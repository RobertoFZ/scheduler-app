Your Data Privacy Rights

We respect your right to control your data. In accordance with Facebook's Platform Policy and applicable privacy laws, we provide you with ways to manage or delete your personal information.

Data We Store

Our application stores the following data related to your use of Facebook Post Scheduler:

  • Facebook User ID (provided by Facebook Login)
  • Access tokens for your Facebook Pages
  • Information about your scheduled posts
  • Any images you've uploaded for scheduled posts
  • Your email address (if you've provided permission)

Options for Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete Your Data Through Facebook

You can revoke our app's permissions directly through Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings > Apps and Websites
  2. Find "Facebook Post Scheduler" in your list of connected apps
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access and delete associated information

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

You can request data deletion by contacting us directly. Please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your Facebook User ID (if known)
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

Send your request to: [Your Contact Email]

Option 3: Delete Your Account

When you're logged in to our application, you can:

  1. Go to Account Settings
  2. Click "Delete My Account"
  3. Confirm your choice

This will permanently delete all your data from our systems.

Data Retention Period

After receiving your data deletion request, we will:

  • Process your request within 30 days
  • Delete all your personal information from our active databases
  • Ensure your data is also removed from our backups during our regular backup rotation (maximum 90 days)

Some information may be retained in anonymized form for analytics purposes, or as required by law, but will not be linked to your identity.

Facebook User Data Deletion Callback

In accordance with Facebook's Platform Policy, our application implements the User Data Deletion Callback API. When you delete our app from your Facebook account, we automatically receive a notification to delete your associated data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion or privacy concerns, please contact us at [Your Contact Email].
